MITIGATION PLAYBOOK: FREIGHT SPIKE & DEMAND SURGE

Event Classification: Sudden Demand Spike / Holiday Season Peak / Flash E-Commerce Events

Historical Reference:
- Diwali Season (India): 200–300% demand spike; 3-week fulfillment window
- Black Friday / Cyber Monday: Global demand surge; container capacity constraints
- Post-COVID Recovery (2021): Pent-up demand triggered 6+ month supply chain strain

Risk Indicators:
- E-commerce promotional calendars (Flipkart Super, Amazon Prime, festive seasons)
- Industry demand forecasts exceeding baseline by >50%
- Freight rate spikes (Baltic Clean Tanker Index, Container rates >$5K/TEU)
- Port congestion reports (>5-day vessel waiting queue)

Impact Window: 2–8 weeks
Affected Assets: Container capacity, warehouse space, transportation modes

=== 3-POINT MITIGATION STRATEGY ===

Action 1: CAPACITY RESERVATION (Execute within 1 week before peak)
- Pre-book container capacity 6–8 weeks ahead:
  * Lock in 30–40% of forecasted volume at fixed rates
  * Negotiate seasonal rate locks with carriers (Maersk, MSC, CMA CGM)
- Cost Impact: ₹50–100 per TEU above spot rates (insurance cost)
- Benefit: Guarantee delivery window; avoid mid-peak rate surges (₹300–500/TEU premium)

Action 2: WAREHOUSE STAGING (Execute within 2 weeks before peak)
- Rent temporary warehouse capacity near ports/e-commerce hubs:
  * Delhi, Bangalore, Mumbai cross-dock centers (2–8 week leases)
  * Staging: Stage 3–4 week inventory buffer
- Cost Delta: ₹200–400K in temp storage
- Reduces port congestion; enables fast last-mile fulfillment

Action 3: DYNAMIC FULFILLMENT ROUTING (Execute within 1 week before peak)
- Activate Regional Fulfillment Network:
  * Direct 40–50% of orders to nearest regional warehouse (vs. centralized distribution)
  * Use air freight + express trucking for tier-1 cities (Delhi, Bangalore, Mumbai)
  * Reserve sea freight for bulk, less-time-sensitive orders
- Diversify carrier network: Add backup carriers (cost: +₹50–100 per shipment)
- Cost Delta: ₹300–600K in premium air/express logistics
- Timeline Benefit: 3–5 day faster fulfillment; 30–40% reduction in delivery delays

=== FINANCIAL SUMMARY ===
Total Mitigation Cost: ₹550K–₹1.2M (pre-season investment)
Additional Revenue Captured: ₹15–40M (via on-time delivery + customer satisfaction)
ROI: 15–30x over 8-week peak period

=== POST-RECOVERY MONITORING ===
- Release reserved container capacity post-peak (avoid sunk cost)
- De-occupy temporary warehouses by week 1 post-season (return lease commitments)
- Consolidate back to centralized fulfillment (4–6 weeks transition)
- Update demand forecast models with actual spike patterns (improve future accuracy)

=== EARLY WARNING INDICATORS ===
Monitor these metrics during Q2, Q3 (pre-peak seasons):
- E-commerce platform promotions calendar
- Carrier rate indices trending upward
- Port queue reports (>3-day waiting time)
- Competitor inventory movements
